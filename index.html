Create Your Own Fragrance (CYOF)

Create Your Own Fragrance is a hands-on experience that introduces participants to the world of natural perfumery and scent creation. Participants will learn scent composition, work with high-quality essential oils, and create their own roll-on or spray-based fragrance.

Beyond crafting a scent, the experience covers essential oil structures, scent layering, and ingredient sourcing. Ideal for hobbyists, wellness seekers, or anyone curious about natural perfumery, it includes a curated fragrance kit and a certificate of participation.

About Us

Atriyat is a luxury Atra brand rooted in traditional Atrary while embracing modern techniques. Our fragrances are crafted with passion, using the finest ingredients sourced from around the globe. With a deep understanding of olfactory artistry, Atriyat delivers elegant, authentic, and unforgettable scents.

As manufacturers and curators of fine fragrances, our mission is to revive the legacy of oriental, floral, and modern blends for Atra lovers worldwide.

The Essence of Atriyat

The essence of Atriyat lies in its harmonious blend of heritage and innovation. Each bottle tells a story — a symphony of notes inspired by timeless traditions and modern elegance. We don’t just create fragrances, we craft soulful experiences that linger in memories.

From exotic ouds to delicate florals, Atriyat celebrates individuality through scent, offering a personal signature to every wearer.

Meet the Founder

Nania Kanaujia, the founder of Atriyat. She has already co-founded a startup named Electron-kits before launching Atriyat. Her journey reflects a deep commitment to creativity and innovation, particularly in the realm of luxury and wellness. Through Atriyat, she channels her vision of combining traditional Atrary techniques with a modern aesthetic, crafting fragrances that resonate with authenticity and elegance. Her entrepreneurial path, as featured on platforms like eGrowth, showcases her determination to build something unique and impactful in the world of luxury lifestyle and scent artistry.
